I know we keep saying our kid is just having night terrors, but I am worried our kid is actually telling the truth of what she is seeing at night. I agree she should stop misbehaving but, I do not want to scare our child to death especially since we did not plant that idea in her head. I know you told me to go talk to a doctor about how I am feeling before you left but, I am worried they will put me into one of those crazy people hospitals , especially not the one in Sans.
